Dance Club Baby Factory

Life is not all fun and games when you're running a for-profit orphanage combined with a turned-up-to-eleven dance club in a residential area.

Sure, the partiers will come and produce your babies for free. And those babies will fetch you a handsome price, especially in today's baby-crazed world. Your job is made even simpler with easy access to drugs.

But getting rich is never as easy as that. Pesky neighbours are always trying to keep a young entrepreneur brother down. Your job is to keep the music contained within your club so they don't call the cops with noise complaints. Partiers will do the dirty work for you.
